令$X = [x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_n, 1]^T$,
$W^\star = [w_1^\star, w_2^\star, \cdots, b^\star]^T$,
因为$yf(x,w^\star) > 0, \forall x$
我们有
\begin{align*}
Y^T(X^T W^\star) > & 0
\end{align*}
令$Y^T(X^T W^\star) = p, p > 0$,
则
\begin{align*}
Y^T X^T w^\star = & p \\
w^\star = & p(Y^T X^T)^{-1}
\end{align*}
故$w^\star$为$X$的线性组合
