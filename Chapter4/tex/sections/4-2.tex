定义XOR问题如下:

输入:
\begin{align*}
x
= \left[
    \begin{array}{l}
        x_1 \\
        x_2
    \end{array}
\right] \\
x_i \in \{0, 1\}
\end{align*}

输出:
\begin{align*}
\hat{y}
= \left\{
    \begin{array}{ll}
        1 & \textrm{if $x_1 \ne x_2$} \\
        0 & \textrm{otherwise}
    \end{array}
\right.
\end{align*}

根据题意, 我们拥有以下模型参数:
\begin{align*}
w^{(1)}
= & \left[
    \begin{array}{cc}
        w^{(1)}_{11} & w^{(1)}_{12} \\
        w^{(1)}_{21} & w^{(1)}_{22}
    \end{array}
\right] \\
b^{(1)}
= & \left[
    \begin{array}{c}
        b^{(1)}_1 \\
        b^{(1)}_2
    \end{array}
\right] \\
w^{(2)}
= & \left[
    \begin{array}{cc}
        w^{(2)}_1 & w^{(2)}_2
    \end{array}
\right] \\
b^{(2)}
= & \left[
    \begin{array}{c}
        b^{(2)}
    \end{array}
\right]
\end{align*}

故:
\begin{align*}
z^{(1)} = & w^{(1)}x + b^{(1)} \\
a^{(1)} = & relu(z^{(1)}) \\
\hat{y} = & w^{(2)}a^{(1)} + b^{(2)}
\end{align*}

则其中一解为:
\begin{align*}
w^{(1)} = & \left[
    \begin{array}{cc}
        1 & -1 \\
        -1 & 1
    \end{array}
\right] \\
b^{(1)} = & \left[
    \begin{array}{c}
        0 \\
        0
    \end{array}
\right] \\
w^{(2)} = & \left[
    \begin{array}{cc}
        1 & 1
    \end{array}
\right] \\
b^{(2)} = & \left[
    \begin{array}{c}
        0
    \end{array}
\right]
\end{align*}

参数为以上解时, 则:
\begin{align*}
\begin{array}{cccc}
    x & z^{(1)} & a^{(1)} & \hat{y} \\
    \hline
    [0,0]^T & [0,0]^T & [0,0]^T & 0 \\
    {[0,1]^T} & [-1,1]^T & [0,1]^T & 1 \\
    {[1,0]^T} & [1,-1]^T & [1,0]^T & 1 \\
    {[1,1]^T} & [0,0]^T & [0,0]^T & 0
\end{array}
\end{align*}
